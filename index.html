Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a facial treatment?

Use the "Book Now" button, fill out the booking form below, or call our reception. We confirm all bookings within 24 hours.

Are treatments safe for sensitive skin?

Yes — we customize all facial protocols after a thorough skin assessment. Patch tests are used where necessary to ensure safety.

How often should I get facials?

We recommend monthly facials for maintenance. For specific concerns like acne or pigmentation, we may suggest bi-weekly sessions initially.

Can I combine multiple facial treatments?

Yes. We design combination protocols based on your skin goals and timing — ask during your consultation for personalized recommendations.

What should I expect after my first facial?

Most clients experience immediate glow and hydration. Some treatments like chemical peels may have a brief recovery period which we'll explain in detail.
